About SchoolMap

What is SchoolMap?

SchoolMap is an interactive campus navigation system designed to help students, faculty, visitors, and administrators easily find their way around the school. With our hand-drawn style map, navigating has never been more fun!

Our Mission

We believe that every student and visitor deserves to feel at home on campus from day one. SchoolMap eliminates confusion and helps everyone find classrooms, offices, restrooms, and facilities with ease.

Key Features

  • ✏ Interactive multi-floor campus map
  • ✏ Route finder between any two locations
  • ✏ Color-coded legend for room types
  • ✏ Kiosk-friendly interface for common areas
  • ✏ Admin tools for map management
  • ✏ Accessible for students, faculty, and visitors
